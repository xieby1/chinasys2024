5.1 Mathematical Model

Observation: To preserve the guest‚Äôs precise exception, practical software-based DBTs tend not to employ optimizations that could potentially break instruction boundaries.

\[ Inflation = \frac{\#insts_{host}}{\#insts_{guest}} \] \[ = \frac{ \sum_i[\mathcal{E} inst_i \times \textrm{inf}({inst_i})] - \sum_j [\mathcal{E} TB_j \times \textrm{opt}(TB_j)] - \epsilon }{\sum_i\mathcal{E} inst_i} \]
